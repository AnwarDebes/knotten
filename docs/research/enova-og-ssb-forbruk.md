## Enova-stotte og SSB-baseline for energibruk (verifisert juni 2026)

Indicative reference material for the development's website and concept documents. All figures are indicative and were checked live in June 2026. Rates and rules change, so re-verify at the source before public use.

### 1. Enova: stottetilbud for energieffektiv bolig og lokal fornybar energi

Support for private homes runs through Enovatilskuddet. Amounts verified on enova.no in June 2026:

- Solcelleanlegg (solar PV): 25 percent of approved invoiced costs, capped at 2,500 kr per kW installed effect. No overall ceiling is printed on the current page; total support scales with system size.
- Pris- og effektstyrt energilagringssystem (battery and load control): 25 percent of approved costs, maximum 10,000 kr. Requires control of at least two energy units (for example hot-water tank, floor heating, EV charging), a working HAN-port adapter or equivalent meter, and certified electrical work.
- Smart varmtvannsbereder (smart water heater): 25 percent of approved costs, maximum 4,000 kr. Shifts heating to low-price hours rather than cutting total consumption.
- Broader measure list (per-measure amounts on individual pages): air-to-water and liquid-to-water heat pumps, varmepumpebereder, akkumulatortank, balansert ventilasjon, solfangeranlegg, wall and roof/loft insulation, energy-efficient windows and exterior doors, and energiradgivning.
- Oppgradering av bygningskroppen (building-envelope upgrade): application deadline 30 June 2026 (confirmed); the maximum amount must be confirmed on the dedicated measure page before citing.

Amounts change (a documented reduction occurred in autumn 2023). Verify every kroner figure at enova.no before publishing.

### 2. SSB: indikativ baseline for energibruk per husholdning

For savings and cost calculators (indicative, label clearly with year):

- 2024 (article, updated Dec 2025): about 14,700 kWh electricity and about 17,200 kWh total energy per household per year. Including holiday homes: about 15,700 kWh electricity / 18,500 kWh total. Electricity was about 86 percent of household energy in 2024.
- 2022 statistic (updated July 2025): 14,964 kWh electricity and 17,518 kWh total energy per household, roughly 13 percent below 2012.
- By dwelling type, electricity, 2022 (verify in StatBank before public use): enebolig 19,676 kWh; vaningshus 20,159 kWh; rekkehus 13,747 kWh; blokkleilighet 8,951 kWh.

Cite the reference year explicitly so the calculator baseline is unambiguous.
